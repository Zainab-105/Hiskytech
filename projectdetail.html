PetGabba - iOS App for Pet Grooming Business

PetGabba is a personalized iOS app developed for a local pet grooming business. It allows pet owners to easily book grooming appointments, manage pet profiles, and access grooming services right from their smartphones. The app is designed to enhance customer convenience by offering a seamless, user-friendly experience for scheduling, tracking, and managing pet grooming needs.

Problem Statement

Customers struggled to book grooming appointments through traditional methods like phone calls or walk-ins, leading to missed opportunities and customer frustration.

Pet owners frequently forgot their grooming appointments or missed regular sessions due to the absence of automatic reminders, impacting the continuity of pet care.

Payment for services was done manually, either in-person or over the phone, creating delays and reducing the overall convenience for customers.

Customers lacked an easy way to track their pets' grooming history, such as which services were performed and when the next grooming session was due, leading to confusion and potential overbooking.

The business did not have a platform to engage customers with promotions, rewards, or loyalty incentives, making it difficult to retain existing clients or attract repeat business.

Solution Provided

Schedule grooming sessions with just a few taps, including selecting preferred dates, times, and specific grooming services.

Users can set grooming reminders based on their pet’s needs, such as when a bath, trim, or nail clipping is due.

Keep detailed records of each pet’s grooming sessions, including services performed, products used, and dates.

Customers can pay for grooming services directly through the app, simplifying the payment process.

A built-in rewards system that encourages repeat customers by offering discounts or free services after a certain number of visits.
